# Sanur Ride Co.

## Company Profile

### Company Overview

**Sanur Ride Co.** is a vehicle rental company based in Sanur, Bali, providing reliable and affordable motorcycle, scooter, and car rental services for tourists, digital nomads, business travelers, and local residents.

Founded in **2019**, Sanur Ride Co. was established with a simple mission: to make transportation in Bali easy, transparent, and hassle-free. The company offers well-maintained vehicles, flexible rental periods, and friendly local support to help customers explore Bali comfortably and safely.
